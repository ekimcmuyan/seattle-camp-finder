How to Use Camp Finder

1

Browse Camps

Scroll through recommended camps for each kid, discover new activities in "New Things to Try", or browse the full list by category.

2

Schedule by Clicking Weeks

Each camp card shows available weeks. Click a week chip to assign it to a kid. Click again to cycle through kids, assign to all, or remove.

3

My Schedule

Switch to the My Schedule tab to see each kid's weekly activities at a glance — organized week by week with color-coded names.

4

Calendar View

The Calendar tab shows a grid of all booked camps across the summer, so you can spot open weeks or overlaps.

⌂

Start Over

Tap the home icon in the top-right to change your school district, neighborhoods, kids, or interests at any time.
